POSITION: Senior Full-Stack Developer

COMPANY OVERVIEW
We are a fast-growing SaaS company building next-generation enterprise software solutions. We're looking for an experienced Full-Stack Developer to join our engineering team and help scale our platform to serve millions of users.

JOB DESCRIPTION
We are seeking a talented Senior Full-Stack Developer with strong expertise in React, Node.js, and modern web technologies. The ideal candidate will have experience building scalable backend systems, designing RESTful APIs, and creating responsive user interfaces. You will work closely with product managers, designers, and other engineers to deliver high-quality features.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

Education:
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field from an accredited state university
• Preference for candidates from public universities (state schools)

Experience:
• 5+ years of professional software development experience
• Strong expertise in React.js and modern frontend development
• Extensive experience with Node.js and backend development
• Proven experience with PostgreSQL or other relational databases
• Experience building and maintaining RESTful APIs
• Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)

Technical Skills:
• Frontend: React.js, TypeScript, modern CSS frameworks
• Backend: Node.js, Express.js or similar frameworks
• Database: PostgreSQL (required), experience with query optimization
• DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines
• Cloud: AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda)
• Version Control: Git, GitHub/GitLab

Architecture & Design:
• Experience with microservices architecture
• Understanding of system design and scalability principles
• Ability to design and architect backend systems
• Experience with load balancing and caching strategies
• Knowledge of security best practices

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
• Knowledge of GraphQL
• Experience with message queues (RabbitMQ, Kafka)
• Familiarity with monitoring tools (DataDog, New Relic)

RESPONSIBILITIES
